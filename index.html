Our Services

Computer Repair

Whether you're dealing with a slow system, broken screen, failing hard drive, or software issues, our expert technicians provide fast, reliable repair services for both Apple and Windows computers. We diagnose and fix hardware and software problems with precision, using quality components to restore performance and extend the life of your device.

Upgrades

Boost the speed, storage, and overall performance of your current computer with tailored upgrade solutions. Whether it's adding more RAM, switching to a solid-state drive, or optimizing your operating system, we help extend the life of your device without the cost of a new one.

Trade-In & Recycling Program

Turn your old electronics into savings. Bring in your used or unwanted devices for responsible recycling, and earn discounts on repairs or future purchases. Our eco-friendly program keeps e-waste out of landfills while giving your tech a second life.

Loaner Computers

We know how important it is to stay connected while your device is being repaired. That’s why we offer complimentary loaner laptops to keep you productive and stress-free during the repair process. Available on a first-come, first-served basis—just ask when you check in your device.

About Us

Satko Cyber proudly serves the NC Triad area, including High Point, Greensboro, and Winston-Salem providing fast, affordable, and reliable computer repair services without the constraints of a physical storefront. Our convenient pickup and drop-off services mean easy, stress-free repairs for you and reduced electronic waste for our environment.

Each year, countless devices are discarded prematurely due to fixable issues. At Satko Cyber, our mission is to change this pattern. Through expert diagnostics, high-quality repairs, and transparent, trustworthy service, we extend the life of your valuable technology.

From broken screens and failing hard drives to sluggish performance and frequent system crashes, we've successfully revived hundreds of devices, helping our customers save both time and money while preventing unnecessary replacements.

Our skilled technicians approach every repair with meticulous care, premium-quality parts, and eco-conscious practices. Whether it's your personal laptop or a critical desktop for your business, we ensure your device is repaired swiftly and responsibly, restoring optimal performance when you need it most.
